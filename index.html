About the Artist

Visions from the High Desert

Based in Silver City, New Mexico, Hilary Klein paints the living world of the Southwest through a psychedelic lens — jackrabbits and coyotes, bumblebees and beetles, rendered in swirling acrylics that pulse with color and sacred energy.

Each painting is a portal. Animals appear not merely as creatures but as spirit guides, each outlined in glowing neon against fields of deep purple, burning red, and desert gold. The work asks you to look twice — and then to keep looking.
